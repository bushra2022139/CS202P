Welcome to the GIK Medical Center Website!

Welcome to the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute's Medical Center, a dedicated healthcare hub committed to the well-being of our community. Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to provide comprehensive and compassionate medical care around the clock. Whether you are a student, employee, or a dependent family member, our team of highly skilled professionals is here to ensure your health and safety. With a ten-bed ward, fully-equipped operation theatre, pharmacy, X-Ray department, and a clinical laboratory offering advanced diagnostic services, we strive to meet a wide range of medical needs. Our team, consisting of medical specialists, gynecologists, physicians, radiographers, nurses, and paramedical staff, works collaboratively to deliver exceptional healthcare services. Additionally, we take pride in our modern maternity unit and emergency room equipped with resuscitation facilities. At the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute Medical Center, your well-being is our priority, and we are here to provide you with quality care in a nurturing environment. Welcome to a center where health meets compassion!

Our Services

Our skilled healthcare professionals conduct thorough blood pressure checks, contributing to the proactive management of cardiovascular health.

Entrust your vision to our experienced optometrists, who conduct thorough eye examinations to ensure the preservation of your visual well-being.

Experience precise and secure execution of minor surgical procedures in our state-of-the-art operating theater.

Safeguard against heat-related concerns with our meticulous heat stroke assessment services, particularly crucial in challenging climates.

Utilize our comprehensive blood count tests to evaluate your overall health, aiding in the early detection of potential medical concerns

Prioritize the well-being of your workforce with specialized employee health services. We focus on regular check-ups and assess hygiene factors, ensuring a healthy and productive work environment.

Proactively manage cardiovascular health with our specialized tests, facilitating early detection and intervention for heart-related issues.

Leverage our diagnostic capabilities for precise urine analysis, providing valuable insights into kidney function and overall urinary system health.

Avail yourself of our advanced imaging facilities, providing detailed X-ray services to facilitate precise medical diagnoses.

Extend your care beyond physical health. Our program includes mental health check-ins for both employees and customers, ensuring a supportive environment for overall well-being.

Rely on our highly trained professionals for prompt CPR administration in critical situations, ensuring optimal cardiac care.

Major (R) Dr. Muhammad Nasir Alvi,IN-Charge Medical Centre,GIK Institute.

It represents our dedication to your health and safety and our commitment to delivering compassionate care and support during your time with us. As the In-Charge of the Medical Centre, I assure you that your well-being is our top priority. Whether you are seeking medical attention, undergoing treatment, or simply looking for guidance on leading a healthier lifestyle, our team of healthcare professionals is here to support you every step of the way.
Our Medical Centre is equipped with quality facilities and staffed by experienced medical professionals who provide personalized care tailored to your needs. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, we strive to ensure that you receive the highest standard of care in a comfortable and nurturing environment.
